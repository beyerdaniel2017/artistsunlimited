Who are we?

Artists Unlimited is a group of young adults looking to connect computer science with the music industry, empowering artists with tools and information in hopes of democratizing the music industry. With years of experience in the music industry, we are trying to enable any artist, big or small, producing from their bedroom or from a studio, so that music speaks for itself.

For anyone that is involved in the music industry, it does not take long to see that the PR and A&R team of an artist plays a pivotal and crucial role in the success of an act. Having members of an artists team who focus on getting the artists heard on all online music platforms, making sure social media profiles are buzzing with engagement, and essentially ensuring an artist stays relevant in the public sphere can be one of the most difficult tasks.

We hope to create tools, plugins, and applications that will make it easier for an artist to be the best they can be online.
