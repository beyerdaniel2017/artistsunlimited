FAQs

How can we help you?

At the core, AU is a team of computer scientists who love music. What started as simple observations led to a startling conclusion of today’s world: artists are unprepared on how to use the internet for their careers. Step into Sound Cloud, Spotify, Youtube for a couple of hours and it will shock anyone how much talent is left unheard. We would like to take step away from the current paradigm, where Radio and Mainstream Music is controlled by a handful of labels and take a step towards a new vision, where artists truly have a chance to be successful independently.
We are dedicated to making sure music undergoing our system is heard. We work with multiple clients on ensuring organic engagement on various SoundCloud, Spotify, Facebook, Twitter, Youtube, and other online platforms.
The goal is to strive for a deep understanding in how the internet can be used to aid artists careers. With this, we will turn these insights into easy-to-use plugins, tools, or applications. We hope that every addition or update to the AU Tools will flatten the music industry a little more, allowing the unheard to be heard.

What is our vision?

We believe in a world where artists focus on making music while we focus on maximizing an artist’s efficiency and effectiveness online. We would like to democratize the music industry. We believe in a world where a bedroom producer can have the same chance of entering charts as a producer signed to a major label.

How does a repost submission work?

If you would like to get your track reposted to one of AU Network’s brands, simply navigate to the Repost Submission page and submit your track. We will be in touch by email as to whether or not the track has been approved.

Can I schedule the promotion date for my track?

Unfortunately, we currently do not offer this. However, we intend on adding this very soon.

What is a premiere or an exclusive release?

Every week, we choose 3 tracks in which we take care of online PR for. After submitting your unreleased track via our premiere submission page, we will notify you of what we believe is the best course of action for your track. We will:
-Find an appropriate SoundCloud Channel to release your track on SoundCloud
-Promote the track on SoundCloud
-Handle administrative tasks to upload the track via Spotify and all major outlets (if the artist is interested)
-Create artwork for the track and receive counsel on the best course of action for engaging fans on all social media outlets
-Review an artists online footprint and offer tips and suggestions for improving an artists exposure on the internet

This is free of charge so submit your unreleased music!

If I am a SoundCloud Channel and I am interested in joining the AU Network, how can I join?

If you would like to join the AU Network, please submit an application in the Apply page. We will be in touch.

As a music curator, what are the benefits of joining the AU Network?

We are not only dedicated in empowering artists directly, but also indirectly. In helping music curators be more effective at releasing music themselves, artists will indirectly benefit. By joining the AU Network, a content curator will have all of the tools and resources to successfully launch any of their musical brands. These are tools and resources we have created over hours of working closely with various online music distributors.
